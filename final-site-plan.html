Website Plan: Web Development Services

Site Name

Proposed Name: Web Development Services

This name was chosen because it is clear, descriptive, and directly communicates the nature of the site. It helps users immediately understand that the website offers services related to building, designing, or maintaining websites. The name is professional, searchable, and relevant to potential clients or users seeking digital solutions.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about web development services, showcase past projects, and offer a way for clients to inquire about or request new websites or maintenance work. It will help establish credibility, provide contact opportunities, and explain available service options.

Target Audience

The target audience includes small business owners, startups, freelancers, and organizations in need of a professional website, redesign, or technical support. The site also appeals to non-technical users seeking easy-to-understand service offerings.

Scenarios

  • "How much does it cost to build a simple website for my business?"
    A potential client wants pricing or package information for basic website services.
  • "Can I see examples of websites you've created?"
    A visitor is evaluating your past work to decide if you're the right fit for their project.

Page Structure (3 Pages Total)

  • Home (index.html)
    Welcome message, overview of services, and highlights of recent work or testimonials.
  • About (about.html)
    Background information on the developer or team, mission, and development philosophy.
  • Services (services.html)
    Detailed list of available services (e.g., website design, maintenance, SEO), with optional pricing or contact CTA.

Navigation

All pages will feature a top navigation bar linking to:

  • Home
  • About
  • Services

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color – Dark Blue (#1e3a8a): Used for headings, strong text, and key elements.
  • Accent Color – Teal Green (#10b981): Used for bullet points, emphasis, and design borders.

Typography

  • 'Poppins', sans-serif: Used for all headings (`h1`, `h2`, etc.). It provides a clean, modern feel.
  • 'Roboto', sans-serif: Used for all body text, paragraphs, and general content for clarity and readability.
  • 'Courier New', monospace: Used for code snippets or technical terms to distinguish them visually.

Design Guidelines

  • Layout: Responsive design using Flexbox or CSS Grid
  • Color Scheme: Professional tones like dark blue and green
  • Typography: Modern sans-serif fonts (see above)
  • Branding: Simple logo and favicon that match the domain and service type

Accessibility & Usability

  • Semantic HTML for headings, navigation, and structure
  • Alt text on all images and icons
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation and forms
  • Readable text with sufficient contrast and flexible sizing
